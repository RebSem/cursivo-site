This draft article will define what makes a CRM truly useful for a car rental company. It will compare generic pipeline software with the real operator workflow of inquiry intake, availability matching, quote preparation, and reservation handoff. It will also highlight why the mismatch creates extra manual work rather than removing it. The final article should help buyers see that this is a workflow design problem, not just a software category choice.

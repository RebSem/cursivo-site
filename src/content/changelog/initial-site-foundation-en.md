The public site moved from a single long narrative into a clearer structure for buyers.

## Added

- a compact homepage focused on the core workflow and manager handoff
- dedicated product routes for deeper operational detail
- separate blog and changelog routes with locale-aware collections
- localized English, Russian, and Arabic paths for every published entry

## Improved

- header and footer discovery for secondary SEO routes
- canonical and alternate metadata for content pages
- public release-note structure for future marketing-site updates

## Why this matters

Potential clients should be able to scan the homepage quickly, then move into deeper pages only when they want more detail. This update makes that route cleaner for both human readers and search indexing.
